Geological patience made tangible.

Pearl, jade, amber, and wood form across years, centuries, and epochs
long before any human decides what they should become.

Here, objects begin not as products but as recordings of time.

---

Objects are temporal companions — shaped by geological gesture,
finished by touch intelligence, designed to evolve alongside
the hands that keep them.

Materials carry biographies older than any civilization.
Each substance arrived at our workshop already
possessing a formation narrative we are not permitted to erase.

Spaces exist where materials can be handled,
examined, and understood before any commitment is made.
Conversations develop slowly here.
