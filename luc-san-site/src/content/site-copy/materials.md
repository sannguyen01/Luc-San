Lục San is material infrastructure, not a label.

Every substance we work with passed through a formation
no human hand could replicate — tectonic pressure,
biological patience, atmospheric fossilization,
decades of cellular growth.

Our role begins where geology ends.
Assessment, form, and surface — three stages
through which raw material becomes finished presence.

The process is not a secret. The hours are counted.
The methods are named. The materials are documented
from formation to keeper.
