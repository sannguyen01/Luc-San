# Lục San — Design System

## Aesthetic Philosophy

The site operates as a **material flagship**, not an e-commerce platform.
Visual language draws from Khaite (cinematic negative space), Toteme (grid discipline),
and Syre (infrastructure framing) — but deliberately avoids being categorized as fashion.

## Core Principles

### 90/10 Negative Space
- Whitespace is content. Every section breathes.
- Hero images occupy full viewport with a single object.
- Copy sections use narrow measure (max 38rem).

### No Product Grid on Home
- Home page presents three philosophical pathways, not merchandise.
- Objects page uses tier bands (horizontal), not catalog grids.
- No "Shop now" buttons anywhere. Links are text-based pathways.

### Monochrome Palette
- Background: `#F5F2EC` (warm off-white)
- Text: `#111111`
- Muted: `#6B6560`
- Accent: `#8C7A6B` (earth tone for links only)
- Optional dark alt: `#111111` background with `#F5F2EC` text

### Typography
- **Display**: Cormorant Garamond (300 weight) — editorial serif for headings
- **Body**: Inter — clean sans for meta text and descriptions
- **Manifesto**: Cormorant italic at large scale for single philosophical lines
- No bold weights. Hierarchy through size and spacing, not weight.

### Photography Direction
- Single objects in vast negative space
- Macro material textures (nacre layers, wood grain, jade translucency)
- Hands in making process — no faces unless contextual
- Natural light, warm tones, no flash

### Forbidden Patterns
- No carousel/slider on any page
- No "Add to cart" buttons
- No pricing on tiles (available on inquiry)
- No social media icon clusters in footer
- No pop-ups, modals, or newsletter captures
- No hamburger icon — mobile nav uses text "Menu/Close"
