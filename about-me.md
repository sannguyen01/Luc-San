## I. What Luc San Is

Luc San creates objects from natural materials — jewelry, furniture, vessels, sculptures — designed to last generations rather than seasons. We work with jade, pearl, amber, rare woods, bronze, silver, and other substances formed through geological time, transforming them through patient handwork into pieces that improve with age and use.

**Brand Essence (One Phrase):** Geological patience made tangible.

**Core Descriptors:** Pure, Poetic, Precious, Pulchritudinous — these are philosophical commitments, not marketing language.

**Positioning:** The third grammar between luxury mythology and mass disposability — substance at honest prices.

## II. Three Business Lines

### A. Luc San Objects (Primary Revenue: 55-65%)

Finished goods sold directly to customers through Instagram, website, and Spaces. Four product tiers:

| Tier | VND Range | USD Range | Artisan Hours | Margin Multiplier | Min. Gross Margin |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Elemental** | ₫1.2M–7.6M | $50–320 | 8–25 hrs | 1.8–2.0x | 35% |
| **Composed** | ₫8M–28M | $350–1,200 | 25–60 hrs | 2.0–2.3x | 42% |
| **Complex** | ₫30M–95M | $1,300–3,800 | 80–180 hrs | 2.3–2.6x | 48% |
| **Commissioned** | ₫100M–420M | $2,200–7,500+ | 120–400+ hrs | 2.4–2.6x + consultation | 52% |

**Pricing Formula:** Price = (Material_Cost + Labor_Cost + Overhead + Design_IP) x Margin_Multiplier

**Tier Boundaries:** Enforced 15–25% price gap between tiers to prevent cannibalization.

### B. Luc San Materials (B2B Revenue: 35-45%)

Wholesale supply of authenticated natural materials to furniture makers, jewelry studios, fashion houses, and designers. Revenue streams include pre-selected material packets, custom sourcing requests, and technical guidance on working with specific substances. This division stabilizes cash flow and builds industry relationships.

- First B2B orders typically ₫12M–35M with 30–45 day payment terms
- After 2–3 successful transactions, quarterly agreements with volume pricing
- Target: 50–120 B2B clients by Year 4–6

### C. Luc San Spaces (Customer Experience: 5-8%)

Small studios (60–100 sq ft) where people handle materials, watch artisans work, and examine finished pieces. Gallery environments prioritizing sensory experience over sales pressure.

- Space conversion rate: 40–60% of visitors buy within 90 days (vs. 2–6% web-only)
- Space visitors exhibit 2.5–3.8x higher lifetime value
- Appointment-based: 45–90 minute sessions

## III. Material Palette & Quality Standards

### Pearls
- Nacre thickness: 0.4mm minimum
- Luster: 7/10+ on GIA scale
- Surface quality: 95% clean target
- Rejection rate: under 5%
- Certification tiered: GIA for >$500 (150–290% ROI), SSEF for $180–$500 (80–125% ROI), in-house grading for <$180

### Wood
- Moisture content: 8–12%
- Drying method: air-dried 18–36 months
- Selection ratio: 200 boards inspected per 8 selected
- Joinery tolerance: 0.5 degree maximum
- Primary species: walnut, white oak, ash, maple
- Occasional species (ethically verified): ebony, rosewood

### Metals
- Silver: 92.5% minimum purity, third-party assay verified
- Gold: 14K–18K, chain of custody certified
- Copper/Bronze/Brass: patina development tested
- Working methods: forging, casting, wire weaving, patination, hand finishing

### Cross-Material Standards
- All materials must pass 5-filter selection protocol: Formation Narrative, Inherent Beauty, Working Compatibility, Temporal Evolution, Ethical Sourcing
- Any synthetic material lacking verifiable formation narrative is disqualified
- Materials requiring deceptive treatments or coatings for appeal are disqualified
